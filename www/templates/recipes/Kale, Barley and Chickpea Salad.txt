START the 1 cup barley (1:2 ratio, simmer)

PREP the 8 cups kale (wash, chop, salt/oil)
MASSAGE the kale

START a mixing bowl
ADD 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
ADD 2 tbsp tahini
ADD 1 tbsp lemon juice
ADD 1 tbsp dijon
ADD 3 tbsp water
ADD 1/2 tsp salt
ADD pinch black pepper

PREP red onions (chop), add to bowl
PREP almonds (chop), add to bowl
PREP chickpeas (open the can, drain, wash), add to bowl

FINISH the barley
DRAIN barley 
RINSE barley with cold water

ADD barley to mixing bowl

SERVE!
