HEAT the pan (olive oil)

PREP 2 red bell peppers (wash, slice, add to pan)
PREP 2 onions (dice, add to pan)
SAUTE until soft 

PREP 4 tomatoes (wash, dice)

PREP 3 cloves garlic (mince, add to pan)
STIR & SAUTE (2 minutes)

ADD 1 tsp cumin to pan
ADD 1 tsp paprika to pan
ADD pinch cayenne to pan
STIR & SAUTE (1 minute)

ADD tomatoes to pan
ADD 1 tsp salt to pan
ADD pinch black pepper to pan
ADD 1/4 cup water to pan
STIR & SIMMER (5 minutes)

PREP 2 cups spinach (wash, chop, add to pan)
STIR & SIMMER (2 minutes)

MAKE 4 wells in sauce
CRACK 1 egg into each well
WAIT while egg cooks

SERVE!
