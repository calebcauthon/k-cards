HEAT the pan (olive oil)

PREP 2 cans chickpeas (drain, wash, add to pan)
PREP cheese (shred)
PREP 1 avocado (sliced)
PREP shredded cabbage (wash, chop)
PREP green onions (wash, chop)

ADD chickpeas to pan
ADD 2 tbsp soy sauce to pan
ADD 1 tsp cumin to pan
ADD 1/4 tsp chili powder to pan
ADD 1/2 tsp ground coriander to pan
ADD 1/2 tsp paprika to pan
MASH up the pan contents
ADD 1/4 cup water to pan
ADD 2 tbsp lime juice to pan
ADD salt & pepper
STIR and remove from heat

RINSE the pan

HEAT the pan (olive oil)

START taco flow
RETRIEVE 1 taco shell
ADD filling
ADD cheese
PLACE flat taco in the pan (1 min)
FOLD taco over when pliable
WAIT 1 min
FLIP
WAIT 1 min
REPEAT 10 tacos
